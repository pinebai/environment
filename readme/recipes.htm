Cheese Sauce not so complicated: Heat about a cup or two cups of milk (depending on how much you want) stir about a tablespoon of cornstarch with a little cold milk then add it to the milk and keep stirring over the heat. It will begin to thicken. Add about a cup of grated cheese and a little salt and pepper and dry mustard (if you have it). It's ready when the cheese melts. If youbuy the corn starch it will tell you the exact proportions on the side of the packet.
Cheese Sauce Complicated: melt about 2 tablespoons of butter over low heat, add about 2 tablespoons of flour and keep stirring briskly until all the flour has been mixed in to the butter. keep stirring or whisking over low heat as you gradually add the milk (about 1 and ahalf cups). Increase the heat a bit and as you stir it will gradually thicken,add the grated cheese and S & P and dry mustard.

Gado- Gado Sauce (for ~1/2 lb Spaghetti)

Ingredients

  • 3tbs +1tsp oil
  • 2 tbs sunflower seeds
  • 1 Tbs dried onion (rehydrated
  • 3 tbs brown sugar
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • c1/2 tsp black pepper
  • c1/2 tsp hot sauce
  • c1/2 tsp spike
  • 3/4 Cup water (or more as needed
  • 3 Tbs vinegar
  • 3 tbs soy sauce
  • 3 tbs peanut butter
  • slice green or wild onions

Preperation

  • Heat 3 tbs oil in frying pan.
  • Add sunflower seeds rehydrated onions, cook and stir over medium heat for 2 minutes.
  • Add base w/ brown sugar. garlic + other spices and 3/4 cup water
  • Add vinegar & soy sauce
  • Add Peanut butter and stir. Active time: 25 min Start to finish: 35 min 1 3/4 cups chicken broth 1 1/2 lb skinless boneless chicken breast 1/2 cup mayonnaise 1/3 cup plain yogurt 5 teaspoons curry powder c 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice 1 teaspoon honey 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 1 medium red onion, chopped (1 cup) c 1 firm-ripe mango (3/4 lb), peeled, pitted, and chopped 1 cup red seedless grapes (5 oz), halved c 1/2 cup salted roasted cashews, coarsely chopped 3 Celery Stalks Bring 4 cups water to a simmer with chicken broth in a 2- to 3-quart saucepan. Add chicken and simmer, uncovered, 6 minutes. Remove pan from heat and cover, then let stand until chicken is cooked through, about 15 minutes. Transfer chicken to a plate and cool 10 minutes. Chop into 1/2-inch pieces. While chicken is cooling, whisk together mayonnaise, yogurt, curry, lime juice, honey, ginger, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Add chicken, onion, mango, grapes, and cashews and stir gently to combine. Makes 6 servings.
